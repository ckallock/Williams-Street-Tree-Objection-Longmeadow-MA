# Williams Street Tree Objection — Longmeadow, MA

A public petition site for residents objecting to the proposed removal of public shade trees on Williams Street, Longmeadow, Massachusetts.

**Legal basis:** M.G.L. Chapter 87, Section 4 — a single written objection submitted at or before the public hearing triggers a mandatory stay of removal, requiring Select Board approval before any tree can be cut.

## Pages

- **[Sign the Petition](index.html)** — Digital signature collection with live signer list and CSV export
- **[Read the Letter](letter.html)** — Full text of the formal written objection

## How to Submit

1. Collect signatures digitally via this site
2. Print the PDF and collect wet ink signatures door-to-door
3. Deliver signed copies **at or before** the public hearing to:
   - Tree Warden Leah Grigorov, Dept. of Public Works, 95 Pondside Road, Longmeadow, MA 01106
   - Select Board, Greenwood Municipal Center, 231 Maple Road, Longmeadow, MA 01106
4. Email a scanned copy to selectboard@longmeadowma.gov same day
5. Attend the hearing in person and speak on the record
